Banyak orang mencampur istilah on-ramp, off-ramp, on-chain, cross-chain, dan swap seolah semuanya sama. Bagi saya, ini sumber salah desain produk.

Miskonsepsi yang Saya Temui di Lapangan

Di banyak implementasi, arsitektur payment dan blockchain digabung tanpa batas yang jelas. Di Findigi, saya membedakan tegas:

  • On-ramp/off-ramp untuk alur fiat-crypto
  • On-chain execution untuk settlement internal
  • Cross-chain untuk interoperability
  • Bridge swap (crosschain-swap) untuk perpindahan lintas jaringan

Kenapa Saya Kritis pada EVM-Centric Mindset

Banyak project bangga memakai EVM di chain lain. Itu cepat untuk go-to-market, tapi sering membuat orang lupa soal kontrol arsitektur, audit boundary, dan dependency governance. Saya memilih membangun KVP sebagai ledger-based protocol agar economic rules tidak rentan.

KVP: Ledger-Based POSA Protocol

KVP menggabungkan Proof of Stake + Proof of Authority (POSA). Tokenomic utama tidak ditaruh di smart contract melainkan di level sistem protokol agar rule enforcement lebih deterministic.

Findigi saat ini masih alpha test. Roadmap: saat KVP mature, core settlement Findigi akan ditarik ke KVP blockchain untuk integrasi vertikal yang utuh.

Kenapa Saya Bangun Findigi dan KVP Bersamaan

Findigi adalah produk validasi use case nyata, sedangkan KVP adalah fondasi jangka panjang. Keduanya satu strategi: product realism + protocol sovereignty.

Why I Built Findigi and KVP: Beyond On-Ramp, Off-Ramp, and EVM Dependency

By Hendri Rahmat Hendrianto • Updated Apr 25, 2026

Many people conflate on-ramp, off-ramp, on-chain, cross-chain, and swap as if they are all the same. For me, this is the source of bad product design.

Misconceptions I Encountered in the Field

In many implementations, payment and blockchain architecture are combined without clear boundaries. In Findigi, I drew strict distinctions:

  • On-ramp/off-ramp for fiat-to-crypto flows
  • On-chain execution for internal settlement
  • Cross-chain for interoperability
  • Bridge swap (crosschain-swap) for cross-network transfers

Why I'm Critical of the EVM-Centric Mindset

Many projects are proud of running EVM on another chain. That's fast for go-to-market, but often causes people to forget about architectural control, audit boundaries, and dependency governance. I chose to build KVP as a ledger-based protocol so economic rules are not vulnerable to manipulation.

KVP: Ledger-Based POSA Protocol

KVP combines Proof of Stake + Proof of Authority (POSA). The core tokenomics are not placed in a smart contract but at the protocol system level, so rule enforcement is more deterministic and harder to manipulate.

Findigi is currently in alpha testing. The roadmap: when KVP matures, Findigi's core settlement will be gradually moved to KVP blockchain for complete vertical integration.

Why I Built Findigi and KVP Simultaneously

Findigi is a product that validates real use cases, while KVP is the long-term foundation. Both are one strategy: product realism + protocol sovereignty.

  • Fiat money
  • On-chain and off-chain transactions
  • Proof of stake
  • Proof of authority
  • Smart contract
